Spatial vizualisation composer project

Basic demo widget

This widget demonstrates the possibility to export geo-datas into svg files, for easy integration in graphic design production. Both the composer and the output can be shaped to suit any kind of needs, from simple shapes to a fully templated design.

Zoom to the zone you would like to export in svg.

Press validate to export the visible countries.

This is a demonstration product from Atelier Cartographique.
